## Section 4: Mobility Metric (Best-Effort)

| Exchange | Top 10 founded after 2000 | Top 10 IPO'd after 2010 |
|----------|--------------------------|------------------------|
| NYSE | 0 | 0 |
| NASDAQ | 2 (Meta 2004, Tesla 2003) | 2 (Meta 2012, Tesla 2010) |
| KOSPI | 3 (Samsung Biologics 2011, LG Energy Solution 2020, SK Square 2021) | 4 (LG Energy Solution 2022, SK Square 2021, Samsung Biologics 2016, HD Hyundai Heavy 2017) |
| KOSDAQ | 6 (EcoPro BM, Alteogen, Rainbow Robotics, ABL Bio, HLB, LigaChem) | 7 (EcoPro BM, Alteogen, Rainbow Robotics, ABL Bio, Kolon TissueGene, HLB, LigaChem) |
| TSE | 2 (MUFG 2005, SMFG 2002) | 0 |
| China A | 2 (CATL 2011, Foxconn II 2015) | 4 (CATL 2018, Foxconn II 2018, China Mobile 2022, CNOOC 2022) |
| HKEX | 0 | 1 (Alibaba 2019) |
| LSE | 0 | 1 (Linde re-listed 2018; Arm NASDAQ-primary) |
| NSE (India) | 0 | 1 (LIC 2022) |

**Key Insight:** KOSDAQ shows the highest mobility -- 6 of 10 top companies were founded after 2000, and 9 of 10 listed after 2010. This reflects the bio-pharma/robotics boom on Korea's growth market. China A-shares also show notable mobility driven by CATL and recent secondary A-share listings of state champions. NYSE and LSE are the most static -- their top 10 consists almost entirely of century-old institutions.
